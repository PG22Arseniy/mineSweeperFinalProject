How to play

Every new game starts with a grid of unopened squares.
The goal is to open all squares on the field that are not mines.
If you open a mine - you lose.
A non mine square displays a number.
It refers to the number of mines that are currently touching the square.

TO OPEN A SQUARE USE LEFT MOUSE CLICK
TO MARK A SQUARE WITH A MINE USE RGHT MOUSE CLICK
